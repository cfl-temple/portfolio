Mr. Richard Lin is an undergraduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Temple University, passionate about robotics, artificial intelligence, and software‑hardware integration. He works as an undergraduate research assistant in the Computer Fusion Lab, where he develops the movement systems for an assistive humanoid robot designed to support individuals with Parkinson’s disease. Richard also manages the lab’s website, serves as system administrator, and is an E-board member of Temple’s IEEE student organization, where he develops leadership skills while guiding technical initiatives and supporting his peers.
