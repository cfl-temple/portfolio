Richard Lin is an undergraduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Temple University and a Temple STEPS Scholar (NSF-supported Sustainable Temple Energy and Power Scholars Program), recognized for academic excellence, leadership potential, and commitment to sustainability and energy innovation. He is passionate about robotics, artificial intelligence, sustainability, and software–hardware integration. Richard works as an undergraduate research assistant in the Computer Fusion Lab, developing movement systems for an assistive humanoid robot for health applications, and also serves as system administrator, website manager, and an E-board member of Temple’s IEEE student organization.
